Mission

IlliniGather's mission is to help students at UIUC discover and engage with campus life effortlessly. We provide personalized recommendations for activities and events based on user preferences and real-time campus information.

Functionality

IlliniGather allows users to input their preferences, such as the number of people, weather conditions, the type of activity, and the time of day. Based on these preferences, our platform generates itineraries that include study spots, restaurants, parties, community/open RSO events, and more.

Users can upvote or downvote locations and mark their presence at those places. The most popular locations will be displayed on the homepage.

We regularly update the popularity of locations in each category to provide users with the most relevant recommendations.
